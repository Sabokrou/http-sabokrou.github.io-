I am a researcher at Institute for Research in Fundamental sciences (IPM). My research focuses on intersection of Computer Vision and Machine learning, especially unsupervised feature learning and one-class classification for computer vision and medical applications. I was also a postdoctoral researcher at Center for Machine Vision and Signal Analysis (CMVS), University of Oulu, Finland, and University of Technology of Troyes, France.

 

I am always looking for new student and researcher for collaboration on following topics:

         Unsupervised or semi-supervised deep learning and its applications

         Anomaly detection in Video/medical/time-series

         Few shot learning, Zero-shot learning and incremental learning

         Security of machine learning algorithm such as defense against adversarial examples.

         Low-power Deep Neural Networks

Send an email if you are interested
